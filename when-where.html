When & Where

Wedding Ceremony

Saturday, March 21, 2026, 3:15 PM - 5:00 PM at San Francisco City Hall

San Francisco City Hall is a grand Beaux-Arts landmark, the seat of city government, known for its stunning architecture, including a vast rotunda and marble grand staircase, and its impressive dome, the fifth largest in the world.

Wedding Reception

Saturday, March 21, 2026, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM at The Good Eating Company

The Good Eating Company is a popular event space in San Jose, known for its innovative cuisine and welcoming atmosphere. It's a modern, clean venue for celebrating the joyous occasion with family and friends.

Directions & Parking

SF City Hall -> San Jose

There is a large parking garage near SF City Hall at the Civic Center. From SF City Hall, it is about an hour drive to the reception location.
